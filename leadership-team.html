Dr. Teni Adesanya

CHAIRMAN - OXFORD GROUP NIGERIA

Dr. Teniola Adesanya is a Nigerian Real Estate mogul, Entrepreneur, Business Coach and Philanthropist. He is the Chairman of OXFORD GROUP NIGERIA, and Founder of TENI AFRICA FOUDATION. He was recognized as one of Nigeria's Top 10 CEO's in 2018 by the Top 10 Magazine. Teni as he is fondly called was born in Lagos, Nigeria. He hails from Itele, Ijebu-East L.G.A of Ogun State. As an enthusiastic learner, he has a B.Sc. in Micro Biology from the Lagos State University, a master's degree in Real Estate management and was conferred with an honorary doctorate degree in Property management and estate management from the European Wealth of Dominica.

He is a seasoned Business man with vast experience in Finance, Investment, Insurance, and Real Estate management. He started his career with Aims Asset Management Ltd. (AAML); A Fund Management Organization, as an Executive marketer and in less than three (3) years, he rose to the post of a Regional manager in one of its South-West Branch. He worked there for 3 years, as a fund Manager and Stock Analyst. In 2010, he left AAML to join UBAMetropolitan. An Insurance Company, as a Business Manager for a year before starting up Pacific Asset Ltd; A Company that was into Oil and Gas Servicing and Finance as the Managing Director.
